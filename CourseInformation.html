ESSE 4600 - Geographical Information Systems (GIS) and Data Integration

About The Course

ESSE 4600 is a project-oriented geomatics course. The course uses GIS systems such as ArcGIS, and mathematical techniques such as map algebraic to integrate diverse datasets.
